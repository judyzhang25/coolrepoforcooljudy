About this page

An increase in a city's population surgest the number of emergency call volume and response rate. Trends in existing data may assist our services in being better prepared and more readily available to handle emergency situations. The SF Data Analytics team has prepared a series of graphs and metrics that serve to provide statistics regarding the quality of current services. This page features topics such as:

  • Facts and Queries
  • Average Dispatch Time vs Time of Day
  • Average Ambulance Response Time vs Time of Day
  • Most Frequent Call Locations
